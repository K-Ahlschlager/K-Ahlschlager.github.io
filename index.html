About

Hello there, welcome to my personal portfolio.

I'm Kyle, a Stellenbosch University Engineering student. I'm currently doing my masters in autonomous robotics under the supervision of Dr Callen Fisher.

My research currently focuses on the design and development of an autonomous system that integrates a standard FPV quadcopter with a RHex-styled ground platform. This research has allowed me to further explore my passion in robotics.

Personal Information.

When I'm not conducting groundbreaking research I am often tinkering with my 3D printers or trying to break the sound barrier with a RC quadcopter.
I do spend time enjoy the outdoors, wether it be bass fishing or practicing archery.

Publications

Publications stemming from my research are shown below.

Using a multi-robot system for improved path planning

The main objective of this paper was to develop a multi-robot system that was capable of using the mobility of an aerial vehicle to guide navigation of a ground-based platform.
Published in 2023 RAPDASA-RobMech-PRASA-AMI Conference.

Tethered Multi-Robot System for Improved Path Planning and Exploration

The main objective of this paper was to develop a multi-robot system that was capable of using the mobility of an aerial vehicle to guide navigation of a ground-based platform. The use of an additional aerial view is used to enhance the navigation of the terrestrial robot and allow it to navigate environments more effectively than if it were to enter it blind.
Accepted into the 2023 iteration of ICAR - International Conference on Advanced Robotics.
